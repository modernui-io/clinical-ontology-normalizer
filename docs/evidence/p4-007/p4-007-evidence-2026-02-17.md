## Pre-Activation Checklist

### 90-Day Stable Baseline Requirement
```
Measurement window: Day 1 of pilot launch -> Day 90
Required metrics:
  - SEV-1 AI-related incidents: 0
  - SEV-2 AI-related incidents: <= 2 (with resolution documented)
  - Confidence calibration drift: < 5% from initial baseline
  - Extraction precision: stable at >85% (30-day rolling)
  - Clinician satisfaction (NPS or equivalent): measured and baselined
```
