## Purpose

Establish a recurring tabletop exercise schedule for business continuity scenarios, with action item tracking to closure.

## Exercise Schedule

| Month | Scenario | Lead | Participants |
|---|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | Database corruption + restore | Operations Lead | CTO, SRE, DBA |

| Month 3 | Complete datacenter failover | CTO | Operations, SRE, Clinical AI |

| Month 6 | Multi-site data sync failure | Interop Lead | CTO, Operations, Data Eng |
| Month 7 | Key person unavailability | CIO | All leads |
| Month 8 | Regulatory audit simulation | Compliance | CISO, CIO, Legal |

| Month 10 | Clinical AI misdiagnosis event | Clinical AI Lead | CTO, CMO, Legal, Product |
| Month 11 | Vendor contract termination | CIO | CTO, Legal, Finance |
| Month 12 | Annual comprehensive review | Program Lead | All stakeholders |

## Exercise Format (90 minutes)

### Setup (10 min)
- Present scenario details
- Define scope and assumptions
- Assign roles

### Walkthrough (45 min)
- Step through incident timeline
- Discuss decisions at each phase
- Identify gaps in procedures

### Debrief (20 min)
- What worked well
- What needs improvement
- Action items identified

### Documentation (15 min)
- Record findings in exercise report
- Assign action item owners and deadlines
- Update relevant runbooks/procedures

## Action Item Tracking

- Action items entered in backlog within 48 hours
- Monthly review of open action items in ops meeting
- Items not closed by deadline escalate to CIO
- Quarterly summary of exercise findings in executive report

| Average time to close action items | <30 days |
| Participation rate | >90% of required attendees |
